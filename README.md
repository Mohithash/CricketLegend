# Cricket Legend

A cricket **player career simulation** for Android (Kotlin + Jetpack Compose). Start as an
18-year-old domestic cricketer, earn national call-ups, dominate franchise leagues and World
Cups, manage your fortune — and chase the real record books.

## Features (v2.7)

- **Sim rest of season** — one tap fast-forwards through every remaining fixture, pausing only
  when an event or auction needs your call. No more click-per-match.
- **Earned nickname** — the media christen you ("The Destroyer", "The Wall", "Boy Wonder"…) based
  on your playstyle and records; shown in the hero header.
- **Retirement testimonial** — a packed farewell match pays tribute with a fat cheque scaled to
  your legacy.
- **Consistent scorecards** (v2.6) — the winning side's total always matches the result.

## Features (v2.5 — UI overhaul)

- **Gradient hero header** on Home — initials avatar, name, live Icon Status, colour-coded
  status chips (Test/ODI/T20I/captain/franchise/contract) and net worth at a glance.
- **Stat-tile dashboard** — a 2×3 grid of headline numbers (runs, 100s, wickets, trophies,
  records, followers).
- **Career graphs** — a cumulative-runs trajectory line chart on Home, a legacy-growth curve on
  the Legacy tab, and live price sparklines on every investment.
- **Visual trophy cabinet** — gradient achievement badges for trophies, awards and life
  milestones instead of plain lines.

## Features (v2.4 — the life of success)

Lean all the way into the fantasy of a legendary life:

- **Icon Status** — an escalating prestige tier with no ceiling: Rising Talent → Superstar →
  National Treasure → Living Legend → Global Sporting Icon → Immortal → **The Cricketing
  Deity**. Shown with a progress bar on Home and the Legacy tab.
- **Grand life ceremonies** — one-time milestone moments as success compounds: first ₹1 crore,
  a wax figure, your authorised biopic premiere, a bronze statue, the **stadium renamed after
  you**, national honour, honorary knighthood, a private island, a billionaire banner, a
  banquet with the head of state.
- **"What comes next" projection board** — the Legacy tab tells you exactly which records
  you're about to break and roughly how many matches away each one is, so there's always a
  next mountain.
- **Fantasy records that regenerate** — smash the 50K-runs club and the bar leaps to 75K; each
  quadruple-century pushes the next tier higher. The chase never ends.
- **Post-retirement second career** — retirement isn't the end: become Head Coach, Commentator,
  Administrator (chase ICC Chairman), Politician, or full-time Business Mogul, and live out the
  years that follow.
- **Dynasty** — your children grow up; continue as your grown child in a New Game+ that
  inherits the family name, wealth, and elite bloodline talent, carrying the legacy across
  generations.

## Features (v2.3 — God Mode & franchise ownership)

- **Format focus** — at creation pick All formats, White-ball only (no Tests), or **T20 & IPL
  only** (the franchise life). Disabled formats never appear on your calendar or in selection.
- **Starting ability incl. GOD MODE** — Rookie / Talented / Superstar / **GOD MODE**. God Mode
  starts with maxed skills so you're *invincible*: a 99-rated bat averages ~175 in T20s, tons a
  century in ~93% of innings, and breaks world records almost every match. Elite stats now
  scale nonlinearly — a maxed player is near-un-dismissable, while ordinary players stay
  realistic (a 60-rated bat still averages ~26).
- **Full franchise ownership** — buy a team for ₹500 Cr, sign marquee players to raise squad
  strength, watch its league campaign play out each season (finish, playoffs, title), and
  **suit up for the team you own as owner-captain**. Titles print money, flops bleed it.
- **Richer media** — pundit verdicts after matches, opposition-star reactions, a live
  record-chase ticker ("just 40 short of the most-ODI-runs record"), and weekly buzz vignettes
  (murals, biopics, trend cycles) that make the world feel alive.

## Features (v2.2 — the life sim)

No ball-by-ball — the game is a **career & life** simulation you drive from the menus:

- **Start at age 9, 14, or 18.** The prodigy path (Vaibhav Suryavanshi style) begins in
  age-group cricket, gets you into a franchise auction as a young teen, and chases the
  **youngest-centurion** records (franchise-T20 at 14, international at 16). Skills blossom
  fastest in the early teens.
- **Playing style** — Aggressive (fearless, high strike rate, more dismissals), Balanced, or
  Defensive (patient, bat time). Changes your scoring profile every match.
- **GOAT meter** — a live legacy score with tiers, a one-time "declared the GOAT" moment when
  you're statistically the best ever, and an **arch-rival** from your generation you're
  measured against for the whole career.
- **Social media** — post training clips (grow followers), run paid brand promos (cash, minor
  image hit), or fire off a controversial hot take (viral upside, troll-storm downside).
- **Dating tiers** — from college sweetheart to movie star; higher tiers boost fame but drain
  faster and invite scandal, with breakups and settlements.
- **Scandals & the dark side** — take a bookie's money for a spot-fixing payday and risk a
  multi-season ban and public disgrace.
- **Mentoring** — established seniors lift a young prospect's game (image + legacy).
- **Own a franchise** — buy a T20 team for ₹500 Cr; title-winning seasons print money, losing
  ones bleed it, all flowing through your ledger.

## Features (v2.1 — simulation depth)

- **Ball-by-ball batting** — actually bat your T20/ODI innings: pick Defend / Normal /
  Attack / Slog each delivery against named opposition bowlers (pace vs spin matchups hit
  your sub-skills), with powerplay and death-over phases, strike rotation, partners falling
  at the other end, and live chases with target and required rate. Quick Sim stays available.
- **A living world** — a cohort of rival stars (Gill, Brook, Ravindra, procedural debutants)
  whose careers advance every season: they contest your ICC awards, raise world-record marks,
  and can *snatch records you hold* — the record book is a race, not a checklist.
- **Sim depth** — choose your batting position (1-8), hot-streak/slump modifiers from your
  last three scores, retire from individual formats at 32+ to prolong the career, rain
  washouts, named injury types.

## Features (v2 — "everything" edition)

- **Live player auction** — bid-by-bid reveal with all 10 franchises (city, captain, colours,
  title history), mega auction every 3 years, retention offers you can accept or reject,
  and the very real possibility of going UNSOLD.
- **Real tournament structures** — league points table with NRR, Qualifier 1 / Eliminator /
  Qualifier 2 / Final playoffs, Orange & Purple Cap races, T20 & ODI World Cups with group
  tables and semis, Champions Trophy (2029, 2033…), WTC Final in odd years, Player of the
  Tournament, single-WC records (Sachin's 673).
- **Deep match sim** — 25 real venues with pitch types (pace/spin/green/flat) interacting with
  vs-pace/vs-spin/power/control sub-skills and bowler type, knockout-pressure nerves, toss &
  pitch report, last-over chase key moments, newspaper front-page headlines.
- **Full economy** — 30% tax on cricket income, 7-instrument investment market (index fund,
  stocks, gold, FD, crypto with crash/moon events), four launchable businesses with flop risk,
  social-media followers that inflate endorsement deals.
- **A life** — dating, marriage, kids, relationship meter strained by touring; press
  conferences shaping a public-image axis; multi-step story arcs (comeback, dressing-room
  rift); national & franchise captaincy; ICC rankings per format with World-No.1 headlines;
  nemesis bowler tracking; milestone matches; awards night (ICC Cricketer of the Year, Arjuna,
  Khel Ratna); a persistent Hall of Fame across careers.

## Core features (v1)

- **Career sim** — domestic first-class/List-A/T20 → T20I/ODI/Test call-ups → franchise
  auctions → World Cups on the real calendar (T20 WC in even years, ODI WC 2027/2031/…),
  retirement + legacy rating.
- **Match engine** — ball-by-ball simulation of your batting and bowling driven by skills,
  form, fitness, morale and opposition strength; scorecards, commentary, Player-of-the-Match.
- **Real data snapshot (mid-2026)** — all 10 Test nations with current stars (Bumrah, Head,
  Brook, Rashid Khan…) as opponents, and a real record book to break: Tendulkar's 100 hundreds
  and 34,357 runs, Murali's 800, Lara's 400*, Rohit's 264, AB's 31-ball century, and more.
  Update `data/RealData.kt` to refresh the snapshot.
- **Money** — match fees, central-contract grades (A+/A/B/C), auction salaries, endorsement
  deals from brands that scale with your fame, a full transaction ledger (₹ Lakh/Crore for
  India, USD elsewhere).
- **Real estate** — 10 properties from a Pune 2BHK to a Maldives island; prices drift yearly,
  rent flows weekly, 3% transaction cost on sales.
- **Lifestyle** — cars, bikes, watches and toys with upkeep costs, fame and morale effects.
- **Invest in yourself** — batting/bowling coaches, trainer, physio and agent in three tiers,
  plus a weekly training focus; all of it feeds skill growth, injury risk and deal sizes.
- **Random events** — endorsement offers, yo-yo tests, tabloid scandals, county stints,
  charity matches, injuries… each with choices and consequences.

## Build

```bash
./gradlew assembleDebug        # APK at app/build/outputs/apk/debug/app-debug.apk
./gradlew testDebugUnitTest    # headless engine tests (pure-JVM, no device needed)
adb install app/build/outputs/apk/debug/app-debug.apk
```

Requires JDK 17 and an Android SDK (compileSdk 36, minSdk 26). SDK path is read from
`local.properties`.

## Architecture

- `model/` — serializable game state (single `GameState`, saved as JSON in app storage).
- `data/RealData.kt` — bundled real-world snapshot: teams, stars, records, brands, market.
- `engine/` — pure-Kotlin logic (no Android imports): `MatchEngine`, `Progression`,
  `Scheduler`, `Finance`, `Events`. Fully unit-testable on the JVM.
- `Game.kt` — global controller; mutates state, bumps a Compose version counter, autosaves.
- `ui/` — Compose screens: Home, Match, Career/Records, Money/Property, Life/Training.

`BalanceReportTest` prints a full 24-season simulated career — useful when tuning the engine.
